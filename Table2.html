 About Ankur Foundation originaly 'Ankur International'
Ankur International was founded on March 28, 2007 with a mission to help 
destitute children and mothers, locally and internationally.
As of now Ankur International is head quartered in Portland, Oregon, USA 
without any branch office anywhere. Ankur International became exempt from
Federal income tax under section 501(c)3 of the Internal Revenue Code since March 28, 2007.
Contributions to Ankur International are tax deductible.
	
Ankur International has partnered with Portland Rescue Mission,
American Red Cross, Braking Cycles, South West Community Health
Center and many other organizations to serve locally.
Our international volunteer team are working hard to support disaster relief,
education, food, shelters and many other critical needs.
